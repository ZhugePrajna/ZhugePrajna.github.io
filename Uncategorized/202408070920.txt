这篇文章也没什么技术水平, 但也算一个小技巧吧

" win + r " and INPUT: "regedit"

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\GameDVR

    Find "AppCaptureEnabled"
    Change "1" to "0"

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\System\GameConfigStore

    Find "GameDVR_Enabled"
    Change "1" to "0"
